How to use the College GPA Calculator

Step-by-step Instructions

1. Enter Personal Info:

Fill in your name, program, institute, and the number of subjects.

2. Add Subjects:

Click "ADD Subject" to generate input fields for each subject.

3. Enter Subject Details:

Input subject name, credit, and score for each subject. The grade field auto-populates.

4. Review and Recalculate:

Check entered details and watch the GPA update as you input scores.

5. Add More Subjects (Optional):

Click "Add New Subject" for additional subjects.

6. View Calculated GPA:

See your GPA displayed at the bottom.

7. Generate a PDF:

Generate your GPA for future reference.
